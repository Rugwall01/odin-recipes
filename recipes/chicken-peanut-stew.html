Chicken Peanut Stew

Description

A savory chicken peanut sauce stew. Peanut sauce with tomato and onion and chunks of chicken served over rice

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Peanut Butter
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Brown the Chicken
  2. Saute the onions
  3. Add everything together in a deep pan or pot
