Kalua Pork

Description

A delicious hawaiian recipe for the whole family. Traditional slow cooked Kalua pork is fall of the bone tender and very juicy. Deep complex flavors from the hawaiian black salt and banana leaves, makes this dish a special treat. Served over rice

Ingredients

  • Pork Shoulder
  • Hawaiian Red Salt or Black salt or comparable
